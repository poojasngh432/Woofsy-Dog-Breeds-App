# WoofsyApp

## Description

**Woofsy** is an app to learn about the most popular Dog breeds, play a fun quiz, see Gifs, and like your favorites to watch later. 
Find it here on playstore: [here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dogbreeds.woofsyapp)

Install it from Google PlayStore
--------------

This is a simple app with user friendly design for all dog lovers. It contains pictures of dog breeds with names, the option to play a quiz to test your knowledge of breed names, or to watch funny gifs of pooches from all over the world. 

You can also download the pictures you like or share with your friends or family, directly from the app.

Save your favorites so you can check later.
